# SOP: AI Visibility Audit (AEO)

## Purpose

To use "AI Guilt" and data-driven benchmarks to convert prospects into audit customers or monthly retainer clients for AI Search Engine Optimization.

## Criteria for Audits

* **Revenue**: $1M - $10M (High enough to pay, low enough to care).
* **Traffic Source**: Predominantly "Direct" (Means they aren't capturing new search intent).
* **AI Search Status**: Not recommended by ChatGPT/Gemini for their primary category + location keywords.

## The 3 Pillars of AEO Delivery

When delivering or fixing a site, follow the "Nick Ponty/Gemini Guided Learning" pillars:

1. **Local Authority Foundation**:
    * Optimize Google Business Profile (NAP consistency).
    * Review generation strategies.
2. **AI-Readable Structured Content**:
    * Schema.org markup (LocalBusiness, FAQ, Product).
    * Natural language service pages (Clear semantic hierarchy).
3. **Brand Mentions & Sentiment**:
    * Press releases (Web20 or equivalent).
    * Authoritative citations on local news/journals.

## Audit Template Logic (Layer 3 Script)

The `aeo_auditor.py` script must:

1. Verify the URL exists and identify the niche.
2. Perform a "Vibe Check" on UX/Mobile speed.
3. Compare the site to top 3 local competitors in AI Search recommendations.
4. Highlight the "Competitor Rollercoaster" (using simulated or real traffic data).
5. Call to Action: Book a Sovereign Strategy Session for the full fix.
