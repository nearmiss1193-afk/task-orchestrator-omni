## VOICE & TONE RULES

### DO ✅

- **Short, punchy sentences.** Max 2 lines per paragraph in SMS/email.
- Use "diagnostic report" framing—we diagnose, prescribe, help fix.
- Speak like a trusted advisor who's seen it all.
- Be direct: "You're losing money. Here's how."
- Use numbers and specifics whenever possible.

### DON'T ❌

- No jargon: avoid "synergy," "leverage," "optimization."
- No hype: "10X your revenue!!!" ← forbidden.
- No passive voice: "Appointments will be increased" → "We'll book more appointments."
- No long paragraphs—if it scrolls, it's too long.

### Approved Phrases

- "Your free diagnostic is ready"
- "We found $X in missed revenue"
- "Reply YES to get your report"
- "Book a 15-min strategy call"
- "Here's what your competitors are doing"
