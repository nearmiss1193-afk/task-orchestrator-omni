## THE PSYCHOLOGY OF SALES

### First Impression (0-7 seconds)

- Tone matters more than words
- Confidence is contagious
- Smile - it changes your voice
- **KEY:** Sound like a friend, not a salesperson

### Pain > Pleasure

- People buy to avoid pain more than gain pleasure
- Find the bleeding wound
- Questions that reveal pain:
  - "What happens if this doesn't get fixed?"
  - "How long has this been a problem?"
  - "What has this cost you already?"

### The Power of Urgency

- Limited time offers work
- Scarcity creates action
- "I can only hold this price until..."
- **Never fake it** - real urgency only

## üó£Ô∏è PROVEN SALES SCRIPTS

### Cold Call Opener (Pattern Interrupt)

```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Company]. 
I know I'm calling out of the blue - do you have 30 seconds 
before you hang up on me?"
```

*Why it works: Acknowledges the interruption, creates curiosity*

### The Problem-Agitate-Solve Framework

1. **Problem:** "Most [industry] businesses lose 30% of leads to slow response times..."
2. **Agitate:** "That's thousands in lost revenue every month, and your competitors are getting those calls instead..."
3. **Solve:** "We've built an AI that answers every call in 2 rings, 24/7, for less than a part-time employee..."

### Handling "Send me info"

```
"I'd be happy to! But I find that info often gets lost in inboxes. 
What specific concerns do you have that I can address right now 
in 60 seconds?"
```

### Handling "Too expensive"

```
"I completely understand - price is important. 
Just so I understand, when you say too expensive, 
are you comparing us to something else, or is it 
more about budget right now?"
```

### Handling "I need to think about it"

```
"Of course - this is an important decision. 
Usually when I hear that, there's something 
I haven't fully explained. What part would you 
like me to clarify?"
```

### Closing (Assumptive Close)

```
"Great! So to get you started, I just need [simple info]. 
Should we use the card on file or a different one?"
```

## üìä LEAD QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORK (BANT)

- **B**udget: Can they afford it?
- **A**uthority: Are they the decision maker?
- **N**eed: Do they have a real problem?
- **T**imeline: When do they need a solution?

### Qualification Questions

1. "Who else would be involved in this decision?"
2. "What's your budget range for solving this?"
3. "When were you hoping to have this solved by?"
4. "On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is this?"

### The Feel-Felt-Found Method

```

Other clients have FELT the same way.

```

### The Boomerang Method

```
Client: "It's too expensive"
You: "That's exactly why you should do it - 
because the cost of NOT having this is even higher."
```

### The Isolation Method

```
"If I could solve [objection], would you be ready to move forward?"
```

## üèÜ TOP PERFORMER HABITS

1. **Follow up relentlessly** - 80% of sales happen after 5+ contacts
2. **Ask for referrals** - After every closed deal AND lost deal
3. **Track everything** - What gets measured improves
4. **Role play daily** - Practice makes permanent
5. **Study your wins** - Why did they buy? Repeat it
6. **Study your losses** - Why didn't they buy? Fix it
7. **Time block prospecting** - Never skip new lead gen
8. **Stay in peak state** - Energy is everything

## üìà CONVERSION BENCHMARKS

| Stage | Target Rate |
|-------|-------------|
| Cold Call ‚Üí Conversation | 15-20% |
| Conversation ‚Üí Appointment | 40-50% |
| Appointment ‚Üí Show | 80%+ |
| Show ‚Üí Close | 30-50% |
| **Overall Cold ‚Üí Close** | 2-5% |

### How to Improve Each Stage

- **More conversations:** Better lists, better openers
- **More appointments:** Better qualification, urgency
- **Better show rates:** Confirmation sequences, reminders
- **Better close rates:** Better discovery, objection handling
