## TARGET VERTICALS (Priority Order)

### 1. HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning)

- **Average ticket:** $5,000-15,000
- **Emergency urgency:** HIGH (no AC in summer = desperate)
- **Seasonality:** Summer/Winter peaks
- **Competition:** Fragmented, slow adopters
- **AI Opportunity:** After-hours emergency booking

### 2. Plumbing

- **Average ticket:** $300-5,000
- **Emergency urgency:** VERY HIGH (flooding = panic)
- **Seasonality:** Moderate (winter pipe bursts)
- **Competition:** Highly local, low-tech
- **AI Opportunity:** 24/7 emergency response

### 3. Roofing

- **Average ticket:** $8,000-25,000
- **Emergency urgency:** Storm-driven
- **Seasonality:** Spring/Fall
- **Competition:** Aggressive sales, high churn
- **AI Opportunity:** Storm follow-up automation

### 4. Electrical

- **Average ticket:** $200-3,000
- **Emergency urgency:** Moderate
- **Seasonality:** Low
- **Competition:** Licensed barrier
- **AI Opportunity:** Appointment scheduling

### 5. Landscaping/Lawn Care

- **Average ticket:** $50-500/month recurring
- **Emergency urgency:** Low
- **Seasonality:** Spring-Fall
- **Competition:** Very fragmented
- **AI Opportunity:** Quote requests, scheduling
